**LLM assessment:** Partially AI-product with métier costume. Strongest tell: Journal `.stat-grid` hero metrics (PRODUCT anti-ref). Warm paper + identical cards + purple Optimiser sticker. Dictation FAB/waveform is authentic, not slop.

**Deterministic scan:** 3 findings — 2× `broken-image` (false positives: dynamic `<img src={...}>`), 1× `single-font` (intentional for product register / DESIGN One Family Rule — false positive).

**Visual overlays:** Skipped — no mutable browser automation in this session. Fallback: CLI detector + source review.
